About Me

Born and Raised in the Philippines from March 13, 1995 to mid to late August of 2012. My paternal family was already in the states at the time when it became time for us to follow. My mother brought my brother and I onto a plane to America, and here we are! I grew accustomed to ways of Union City, CA very quickly. Strange and unfamiliar roads soon became home to me. I made friends with the neighbourhood kids and spent the rest of the summer playing with them everyday until the school year began. I started my new life as a 1st grader at Alvarado Elementary where my efforts from my private preschool became handy. My English would progress so fast that not only would my marks be close to perfect, but my native language would become lost to me at home. While I would still consider myself to be bilingual it could definitely be better. With that being said, I at the ripe age of 27 am bilingual, being able to understand and speak both Engligh and Tagalog at a proficient level. Fast forward to adulthood, I went to school for my nursing license in 2018. Though it was most definitely a struggle I prevailed a year later and acheived the status of LVN. It's nothing fancy, yes, but it's definitely kept a roof over my head and food in my tummy. I'm currently working as a COVID nurse for Santa Clara County in California, while simultameously learning about Web Development.
